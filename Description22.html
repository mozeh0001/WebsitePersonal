Power Saving Robot (PSR)

Adafruit | C++ | IoT | XCode for App Dev | Recognition Using ML + OpenCV

The Power Saving Robot (PSR) is a self driving bot that saves energy by automatically turning off TVs when no one is present in the room. It integrates mmWave presence detection, an IR Blaster for infrared signal transmission, and a Swift iOS app with ML-based TV detection. When the system detects no occupancy, the robot navigates using ultrasonic sensors and actuates servos to press the TV power button.


Key Features

  • Occupancy Detection: mmWave radar checks every 9 seconds to verify presence.
  • Infrared Transmission: IR Blaster mounted on top sends power-off signals to TVs.
  • Smart Behavior: Stops movement when people return; resumes after no presence.
  • Wireless Communication: Bluetooth connection with iOS Swift app.
  • Servo Mechanism: Two servos align and press the TVâ€™s power button when needed.
  • Obstacle Avoidance: Three ultrasonic sensors (front, left, right) guide navigation.
  • Testing Phase: System currently in prototype testing with working IR + presence logic.
