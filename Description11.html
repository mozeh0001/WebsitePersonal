Dad's Legacy

Partner-Based | Personal Project | Unity | C# | Blender | Iot Particle Cloud

Collaborating with a 3D Generalist and Artist, this game integrates IoT play to immerse players in a childlike experience, where they interact with toys and the environment in a tactile and imaginative way. Using Unity and C#, the game incorporates special hardware such as an IoT glove, allowing players to physically manipulate objects, triggering unique in-game effects. Designed to evoke curiosity and wonder, the gameplay encourages experimentation with different toys, each featuring distinct mechanicsâ€”such as sticky hands that grab distant objects or kaleidoscopic lenses that distort reality. By blending interactive technology with surreal, optical illusiory environments, the game promotes a sense of discovery, playful exploration, and nostalgia, simulating how a child perceives and engages with the world. This game takes its inspirations from Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory, Cruelty Squad and Mr. Wondertainment Inc. from the SCP fandom.

IoT Integration

Partner-Based | Personal Project | Unity | C#

We want to bring real-world physicality into our game by integrating IoT-controlled toys, using a glove equipped with pressure sensors and an accelerometer to control an in-game hand rig. The glove will have five pressure sensors on the fingers and one on the wrist, allowing players to interact with virtual objects by gripping, squeezing, or tapping. Additionally, an accelerometer will track hand movements in 3D space, enabling natural motions like tilting, swiping, or lifting objects. This setup will create a seamless connection between physical actions and in-game interactions, making each toy feel uniquely responsive.



Toy Example

For example, a sticky-fingered toy could swing based on the player's real-life gestures, reacting to grip strength and movement. This IoT integration will enhance immersion, making each toy feel tactile, playful, and truly part of the game world.



In the following video, the toy stretches based on how fast it moves, demonstrating its elasticity and dynamic motion. In the future, the IoT glove will allow players to control this interaction directly, using finger pressure sensors and motion tracking to simulate realistic stretching and swinging in-game with Unity's physics-based ineractions.

Optical Illusiory Enviornments

Non-Euclidean spaces and mind-bending visual distortions create a surreal, dreamlike world that challenges players' perception of reality.

Infinite Vortex

  • Endless Swirling Effect: A constantly rotating vortex that gives the illusion of an infinite portal.
  • Depth Illusion: The effect creates a sense of falling or being pulled into another dimension.
  • Dynamic Animation: The vortex moves and twists over time, making it feel alive and ever-changing.
  • Hypnotic Visuals: The swirling motion draws the player's eye, reinforcing the feeling of entering a surreal space.
  • Non-Euclidean Aesthetics: Warped space and shifting geometry add to the illusion of breaking reality.
  • Customizable Speed & Intensity: The effect can be adjusted to create subtle movement or rapid, chaotic spirals.
  • Seamless Transition: Blends naturally with the surrounding environment, making the portal feel immersive.
