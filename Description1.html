Bluetooth Car with Adafruit & Particle Argon

Adafruit | C++ | Particle IoT

This Bluetooth-controlled car is powered by Particle Argon and the Adafruit Bluefruit App, allowing seamless wireless control. The project leverages real-time Bluetooth communication, enabling smooth and responsive navigation.





  • Controlled via the Adafruit Bluefruit App interface
  • Powered by Particle Argon microcontroller
  • Supports real-time Bluetooth communication
  • Designed for smooth navigation and responsiveness
