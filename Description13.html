Frogger

Frogger Recreation

  • Level Parsing & Setup: The game loads level data from text files, ensuring that the initial positions of the Frog, Vehicles, and Logs are set correctly.
  • Frog Movement: The Frog moves using the WASD keys, with movement triggered on the leading edge of key presses. It is restricted from hopping off-screen.
  • Vehicle & Log Movement: Vehicles and Logs move using the WrappingMove function, with each row alternating its movement direction.
  • Collision & Death: The Frog dies upon colliding with a Vehicle, creating a DeadFrog object upon death.
  • Intelligent Vehicles: Vehicles slow down when the Frog is directly in front of them. Detection is implemented using the dot product and arccos calculations.
  • Log Riding: The Frog moves along with logs using the GetMinOverlap function, following the log's speed and direction.
  • Goal & Win Condition: The Frog pauses upon reaching the goal but dies if it attempts to jump into walls or misses logs while crossing.
